Dream Travel Destination Landing Page

Jeju Island

For this challenge I chose a destination I've visited twice before and would love to visit again: Jeju Island in South Korea. I chose 4 major attractions in Jeju province and included some basic information and photos for each, as well as links to more resources. At the top of the page, there is a form asking you what you'd like to do. If your answer includes a keyword associated with one of the 4 attractions I chose, the page will scroll to the relevant section.
